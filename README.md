## 🧠 About Me

> *"Pragmatism over hype — I apply AI where it actually solves problems."*

I'm an **AI & Automation Engineer** at **Labster**, building AI-powered systems that automate internal workflows at scale. My background spans Computer Vision, Deep Learning, MLOps, and low-code automation.

- 🤖 Currently automating education workflows with **n8n**, **Claude** & **OpenAI** at [@Labster](https://www.labster.com/)
- 👁️ Former CV Engineer — multi-camera tracking, thermal imaging, embedded AI (YOLO, SSD, ROS)
- 🎓 MSc in AI & Software Engineering — thesis: *3D MRI Super-Resolution with GANs* (honors)
- 🎤 Speaker at **PyData Granada**, **TechFest 2024**, **TabularConf** & more
- 🏫 Co-founded **[Málaga AI Association](https://twitter.com/aimalaga)** — 200+ students per session
- 🚀 Also built my own FPV Racing Drone. Because why not.
